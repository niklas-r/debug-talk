console.group

console.group(object[, object, ...])

Starts a new logging group with an optional title. All console output that occurs after calling this method and calling console.groupEnd() appears in the same visual group.

console.groupCollapsed(object[, object, ...]) creates a new logging group that is initially collapsed instead of open, as with console.group().

console.groupEnd()

Closes the most recently created logging group that previously created with console.group() or console.groupCollapsed().
